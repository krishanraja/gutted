## Unique Value vs. Alternatives

### vs. Generic Symptom Trackers (Cara, Bowelle)
- **They offer:** Manual symptom logging with basic charts
- **gutted. adds:** Voice-first input, AI analysis per entry, gut scoring, and the critical link to nutrition

### vs. Diet/Meal Plan Apps (MyFitnessPal, Noom)
- **They offer:** Calorie counting and generic meal templates
- **gutted. adds:** Meal plans personalized to your gut profile, symptom patterns, and medical test data

### vs. Medical Test Interpreters (standalone)
- **They offer:** One-time interpretation of a single test
- **gutted. adds:** Continuous integration of test data with daily symptoms and ongoing meal planning

### vs. Seeing a Specialist
- **Specialist provides:** Expert diagnosis and treatment plans
- **gutted. provides:** Day-to-day tracking, pattern recognition, and dietary guidance between visits -- making specialist appointments more productive

## The Compound Effect

```

Week 3:  First meal plan → Personalized to your unique profile
```

The more you use gutted., the more it knows about your gut, and the more personalized every recommendation becomes. This creates a **data moat** that makes switching costs high and retention strong.
