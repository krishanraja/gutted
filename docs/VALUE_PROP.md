## Unique value vs. alternatives

### vs. Generic symptom trackers (Cara, Bowelle, mySymptoms)
- **They:** Manual logging, basic charts, no AI.
- **gutted.:** Voice-first, AI score per entry, meal plan integration, document intelligence, AI coach.

### vs. Diet / meal-plan apps (MyFitnessPal, Noom, ZOE)
- **They:** Calorie counting, generic templates, or member-cohort programs.
- **gutted.:** Plans grounded in *your* gut profile, *your* recent symptoms, *your* uploaded biomarkers.

### vs. Standalone test interpreters (single-result PDFs)
- **They:** One-time interpretation of one test.
- **gutted.:** Continuous integration of test data with daily symptoms and ongoing meal planning.

### vs. Seeing a specialist
- **Specialist:** Diagnosis, treatment plan, prescriptions, 30 minutes per quarter, ~$300/visit.
- **gutted.:** Daily tracking, pattern recognition, dietary guidance, doctor-summary generation -- $14-29/mo, 365 days/year. Makes the specialist visits more productive, not less.

### vs. ChatGPT
- **ChatGPT:** Generic answers, no memory, no data, no health-safety guardrails, no flagged-symptom routing.
- **gutted.:** Domain-tuned prompts grounded in your structured logs and uploads, with prompt-injection delimiters and AI-safety calibration ([LLM_CRITICAL_THINKING_TRAINING.md](./LLM_CRITICAL_THINKING_TRAINING.md)).
